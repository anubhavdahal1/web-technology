Nihareeka College of Management and IT

Description

Nihareeka College of Management and IT is a reputed educational institution in Biratnagar, Nepal. The college offers high-quality programs in Management, Information Technology, Hospitality, and Science with modern labs, experienced faculty, and excellent academic support.

  • Programs: BIM, BBA, BCA, BHM, BSc. CSIT
  • Affiliation: Tribhuvan University
  • Modern Computer & Networking Labs
  • Library & Reading Hall
  • Workshops, Events & Seminars
  • Internship & Career Guidance
  • Located in Biratnagar, Morang
